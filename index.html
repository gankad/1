About Us

Terra Verde Innovations is based in Maharashtra, India. We are composed of Creative Engineers, Professionals and Visionaries, who believe in envisioning futuristic technologies and transforming them into reality.

Products & Services

What We Do?

We bring cutting edge, latest technology to replace conventional and resource hungry energy generation technology. Our products include but not limited to Solar Water Heaters, Solar Power Systems and Wind Power Solutions.

Solar Water Heaters

We provide Solar Water Heaters - ETC & Flat Panels from 100ltrs and above. Please contact us for more information.

Solar Power Systems

We provide domestic as well as industrial use Solar Power Systems ranging from 100 watts to 1000 kilo watts.

Wind Power Solutions

Our Wind Power Solutions are suitable for small households as well as large industries. We are soon going to launch blade-less wind power technology.

Business Services

We also provide ERP/CRM software implementation services. Please contact us for more details.
